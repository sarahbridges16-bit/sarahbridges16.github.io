🏞️ Explore Outdoors

Fresh air, beautiful scenery, and adventures at your own pace.

Spend More Time Outside

Some of the best days begin with a leash, comfortable shoes, and no real schedule. Whether you're exploring a quiet state park, following a lakeshore trail, or discovering a scenic overlook, getting outside is one of the easiest ways to create lasting memories together.

The destinations below were chosen because they offer beautiful scenery and flexibility. You can often decide how long to stay, how far to walk, and how fast to move, making them great choices for a relaxed day outdoors.

Featured Destinations

Devil's Lake State Park

Known for its dramatic bluffs and crystal-clear lake, Devil's Lake is one of Wisconsin's most popular parks. While many visitors head straight for the bluff trails, the lakeshore path offers equally beautiful scenery with a much gentler walk.

Perfect For

Pace
Flexible
Terrain
Paved paths with some packed gravel.
Best Time to Visit
Spring, Summer, and Fall.
Leash Notes
The lakeshore trail offers beautiful views without the steep climb to the bluffs.

Governor Dodge State Park

Governor Dodge is a destination that can easily fill an entire afternoon. Between the walking trails, picnic areas, and scenic lake views, it's a wonderful place to enjoy nature without feeling rushed.

Best Time to Visit

Leash Notes
Bring a picnic and plan extra time to enjoy the lakeshore.

Parfrey's Glen State Natural Area

Parfrey's Glen offers a shorter hike with unique rock formations, towering cliffs, and a peaceful stream. It's a great choice if you're looking for a nature experience without spending the entire day on the trail.
