Little Paws, Big Adventures

Exploring Wisconsin one little adventure at a time.

Welcome!

Hi! We're Sarah, Ryan, Ollie, and Lily. We created Little Paws, Big Adventures after realizing that many dog-friendly travel guides are written with larger, high-energy dogs in mind. As owners of two Cavapoos, we've found ourselves searching for places where we can enjoy the outdoors at a slower pace while making sure our dogs are comfortable too.

This website isn't about finding the biggest hike or the most adventurous trail. It's about discovering meaningful ways to spend time together—whether that's walking through a state park, enjoying a lakeside picnic, wandering a farmers market, or finding a quiet patio for lunch.

Explore Wisconsin Your Way

No matter what kind of day you're planning, we've organized our favorite ideas into four simple categories.

  • 🏞️ Explore Outdoors — Nature trails, scenic overlooks, state parks, beaches, and beautiful places to enjoy the outdoors.
  • ☕ Slow Days — Picnic spots, botanical gardens, coffee shops, patios, and relaxing afternoons.
  • 🎉 Seasonal Favorites — Apple orchards, pumpkin patches, holiday lights, festivals, and traditions worth revisiting every year.
  • ✨ Special Occasions — Date ideas, birthdays, anniversaries, photo-worthy locations, and memorable adventures.
